ABOUT CCOE


City college of Engineering, Mumbai is one of the highly reputed Engineering College in Maharashtra, India offering various courses. Established in year 1999 . The institute offers quality technical education.Our institution was built on a simple yet ambitious premise: that engineering is not just a degree, but a mindset dedicated to solving the world’s most complex challenges.The vision of City College of Engineering is to emerge as a globally recognized center of excellence in technical education, research, and innovation.
