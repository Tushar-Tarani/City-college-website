1. The Core Philosophy (Overview & Leadership)

  • About the Department:

    • Welcome to the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at City college of Engineering. Established in 1999, our department is dedicated to the study and advancement of core electronics, communication networks, and signal processing. We focus on equipping students with the robust hardware and software skills required to design modern communication systems, embedded devices, and next-generation connectivity solutions.


  • Vision:

    • To be a premier center of excellence in Electronics and Telecommunication education and research, producing innovative engineers capable of driving advancements in global communication and smart technologies.


  • Mission:

    1. To deliver a strong, foundational curriculum that seamlessly integrates electronic hardware design with advanced telecommunication networks.
    2. To foster hands-on learning through modern laboratories equipped with industry-standard measurement and simulation tools.
    3. To promote innovative research in areas like 5G/6G networks, VLSI design, and the Internet of Things (IoT).
    4. To build strategic partnerships with the telecom and semiconductor industries to enhance student readiness and employability.



  • Message from the Head of Department:

    • "Welcome to the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication! We live in a hyper-connected world driven by smart devices and rapid communication networks. Our department provides the perfect ecosystem to understand and build the technology that connects us all. From designing intricate microchips to planning vast wireless networks, our faculty and facilities are here to help you turn complex theories into working, real-world systems. Join us in engineering the future of global connectivity." — [HOD Name], Head of Department

2. Academic Programs & Specializations

  • Programs Offered:

    1. B.Tech / B.E. in Electronics and Telecommunication (4-Year Undergraduate Program)
    2. M.Tech / M.E. in Communication Engineering / VLSI Design (2-Year Postgraduate Program)
    3. Ph.D. in Electronics and Telecommunication (Doctoral Research Program)



  • Core Curriculum Focus:

    • Our curriculum is structured to build a deep understanding of both electronics and communication. Key core areas include Analog and Digital Circuits, Signals and Systems, Microcontrollers, Electromagnetic Engineering, and Digital Communication.


  • Project-Based Learning:

    • We bridge theory and practice. Every semester includes hands-on hardware implementations, culminating in a final-year capstone project where students design functioning electronic prototypes, antenna models, or communication protocols to solve real-world industry challenges.

3. Infrastructure & Facilities

  • State-of-the-Art Laboratories:

    • Our department features highly specialized laboratories designed for comprehensive hardware and signal testing:
    1. VLSI & Embedded Systems Lab: Equipped with FPGA boards (like Xilinx or Altera) and ARM Cortex microcontrollers for advanced chip design and embedded programming.
    2. Communication & Microwave Lab: A dedicated space featuring spectrum analyzers, microwave test benches, and antenna training systems.
    3. Digital Signal Processing (DSP) Lab: Geared toward processing audio, video, and data signals using advanced DSP kits.
    4. Electronics Devices & Circuits Lab: Featuring digital storage oscilloscopes (DSOs), function generators, and power supplies for core circuit building.



  • Library & Resources:

    • The department maintains a dedicated resource center with access to thousands of component datasheets, microprocessor manuals, and premium digital access to the IEEE Communications Society journals.

4. Industry Connect & Placements

  • Top Recruiters:

    • Our graduates are highly sought after by both core electronics and IT sectors. We proudly place students in leading telecom giants, semiconductor firms, and networking companies, including e.g., Cisco, Qualcomm, Ericsson, Jio, Airtel, Texas Instruments.


  • Internship Opportunities:

    • Our dedicated placement cell facilitates mandatory 6-month internships, ensuring students gain critical on-ground experience in telecom exchanges, semiconductor fabrication units, or embedded systems companies.


  • Alumni Network:

    • Our strong alumni network spans the globe, with professionals working in top-tier telecom and network infrastructure roles. They actively engage with the department to conduct technical workshops, offer mentorship, and provide direct recruitment referrals.
