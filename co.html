1. The Core Philosophy (Overview & Leadership)

  • About the Department:

    • Welcome to the Department of Computer Engineering at City college of Engineering. Established in 1999, our department is dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical computer science and practical software engineering. We focus on equipping students with the analytical, programming, and problem-solving skills necessary to tackle complex global challenges in a rapidly evolving digital era.


  • Vision:

    • To be a premier center of excellence in Computer Engineering education, research, and innovation, producing globally competent professionals who drive technological advancement and societal growth.


  • Mission:

    1. To deliver a rigorous, industry-aligned curriculum that emphasizes both theoretical foundations and hands-on practical skills.
    2. To foster a culture of research, innovation, and lifelong learning among students and faculty.
    3. To instill strong ethical values, leadership qualities, and a sense of social responsibility.
    4. To build strong partnerships with the tech industry to enhance student employability and entrepreneurial spirit.



  • Message from the Head of Department:

2. Academic Programs & Specializations

  • Programs Offered:

    1. B.Tech / B.E. in Computer Engineering (4-Year Undergraduate Program)
    2. M.Tech / M.E. in Computer Engineering (2-Year Postgraduate Program)
    3. Ph.D. in Computer Engineering (Doctoral Research Program)



  • Core Curriculum Focus:

    • Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to build a rock-solid foundation in computer science. Key core areas include Data Structures and Algorithms, Object-Oriented Programming, Operating Systems, Database Management Systems (DBMS), and Computer Networks.


  • Project-Based Learning:

    • We move beyond the textbook. Every semester integrates hands-on mini-projects, culminating in a final-year capstone project where students solve real-world industry problems, often in collaboration with our corporate partners.

3. Infrastructure & Facilities

  • State-of-the-Art Laboratories:

    • Our department houses highly specialized laboratories designed for modern computing needs:
    1. AI & Deep Learning Lab: Equipped with high-end GPUs for training complex machine learning models.
    2. Network & Cybersecurity Lab: A dedicated environment for simulating network architectures and practicing ethical hacking.
    3. Software Engineering & Testing Lab: Geared toward full-stack web and mobile application development.
    4. IoT & Maker Space: Featuring Raspberry Pi, Arduino kits, and sensors for hardware-software integration.



  • Library & Resources:

    • Beyond the central college library, the department maintains a specialized reading room and provides 24/7 digital access to top-tier academic journals via IEEE Xplore and the ACM Digital Library.

4. Industry Connect & Placements

  • Top Recruiters:

    • Our graduates are highly sought after by the industry. We proudly place students in leading multinational corporations including Google, Microsoft, Amazon, TCS, Infosys, Wipro as well as high-growth tech startups.


  • Internship Opportunities:

    • Our dedicated departmental placement cell facilitates mandatory e.g., 6-month industry internships, ensuring students gain invaluable practical exposure and networking opportunities before graduation.


  • Alumni Network:

    • Our active, global alumni network is one of our greatest strengths. Alumni regularly return to provide one-on-one mentorship, deliver guest lectures on industry trends, and offer direct referral opportunities for current students.
