Mechanical Engineering


1. The Core Philosophy (Overview & Leadership)

  • About the Department:

    • Welcome to the Department of Mechanical Engineering at City college of Engineering. Established in 1999, our department is dedicated to the core principles of design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. We focus on equipping students with a robust foundation in thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and material science, combined with modern digital tools to solve complex engineering challenges.


  • Vision:

    • To be a premier center of excellence in Mechanical Engineering education and research, producing globally competent engineers who innovate sustainable, efficient, and advanced mechanical systems.


  • Mission:

    1. To deliver a rigorous academic curriculum that perfectly balances core physical principles with modern computational design and manufacturing.
    2. To foster hands-on learning through extensive workshop practices and advanced material testing laboratories.
    3. To promote research in renewable energy, thermal optimization, and advanced manufacturing processes.
    4. To build strategic partnerships with the automotive, aerospace, and heavy machinery industries to enhance student employability.



  • Message from the Head of Department:

    • "Welcome to the Department of Mechanical Engineering! Often considered the mother of all engineering branches, mechanical engineering is an ever-evolving field. From designing high-performance automobile engines to developing sustainable renewable energy systems, our discipline shapes the physical world. With our dedicated faculty and industry-standard workshops, we provide the ultimate environment for you to conceptualize, design, and build the machines of tomorrow." — [HOD Name], Head of Department

2. Academic Programs & Specializations

  • Programs Offered:

    1. B.Tech / B.E. in Mechanical Engineering (4-Year Undergraduate Program)
    2. M.Tech / M.E. in Thermal Engineering / Machine Design (2-Year Postgraduate Program)
    3. Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering (Doctoral Research Program)



  • Core Curriculum Focus:

    • Our curriculum is structured to build a deep understanding of physical systems and mechanics. Key core areas include Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Strength of Materials, Kinematics and Dynamics of Machinery, Heat Transfer, and Manufacturing Processes.


  • Project-Based Learning:

    • We strongly emphasize bringing designs to life. Every semester involves practical mechanical drafting and physical fabrication, culminating in a final-year capstone project where students design, analyze, and manufacture working mechanical prototypes or thermal systems.

3. Infrastructure & Facilities

  • State-of-the-Art Laboratories:

    • Our department features expansive, heavy-duty laboratories designed for comprehensive mechanical testing and manufacturing:
    1. CAD/CAM/CIM Lab: Equipped with high-performance workstations for computer-aided design, manufacturing, and integrated modeling.
    2. Fluid Mechanics & Machinery Lab: A dedicated space featuring Pelton wheels, Francis turbines, and advanced flow measurement devices.
    3. IC Engines & Thermodynamics Lab: Geared toward performance testing and emission analysis of internal combustion engines and refrigeration systems.
    4. Advanced Workshop & Machine Shop: Featuring CNC lathes, milling machines, and modern welding stations for extensive fabrication training.



  • Library & Resources:

    • The department maintains a dedicated resource center with comprehensive access to ASME (American Society of Mechanical Engineers) journals, material data handbooks, and standard codes for mechanical design.

4. Industry Connect & Placements

  • Top Recruiters:

    • Our graduates are highly sought after by core manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and energy sectors. We proudly place students in leading corporations including e.g., Larsen & Toubro (L&T), Tata Motors, Mahindra & Mahindra, Godrej, Bosch, Thermax.


  • Internship Opportunities:

    • Our dedicated placement cell facilitates mandatory 6-month internships, ensuring students gain vital on-ground experience in manufacturing plants, power generation facilities, or industrial design firms.


  • Alumni Network:

    • Our strong alumni network spans the globe, with professionals working in top-tier design, production, and managerial roles. They actively engage with the department to conduct technical seminars, offer mentorship, and provide direct recruitment referrals.
